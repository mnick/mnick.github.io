Hi, I'm Max

Since 2016, I am a research scientist (manager) at FAIR, Meta AI where I have also acted research area lead for Machine Learning and Society & Responsible AI. Before joining FAIR, I was lucky to learn from Tomaso Poggio and Lorenzo Rosasco as a postdoctoral fellow at MIT where I was with the Laboratory for Computational and Statistical Learning and the Center for Brains, Minds and Machines. I have received my PhD with summa cum laude from the Ludwig Maximilian University Munich as a research assistant at Siemens Corporate Technology under the guidance of the one and only Volker Tresp.
